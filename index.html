About Teddy Afro and Abugida Band

Teddy Afro is an Ethiopian singer, songwriter and music producer. Abugida Band is a band that collaborates with Teddy Afro. They are famous for their unique blend of traditional Ethiopian music with contemporary elements. They have performed in many countries and have a huge fan base.
